About Me

My road from Zero to Junior Front-end Developer

Hello again, I am George a Junior Front-end Developer. I study at SCSA and I try to grow both professionally and mentally on a daily basis.

If you had asked me about this profession 2 years ago you probably could not say anything, however in a very short time everything changed when one day a friend of mine advised me to try it and just listened to a free course which had a wayup for that period.

I too started listening to lectures and suddenly for me while doing my very first lecture assignment the first hello world felt like a hacker.

My road

February 7

My friend advises me to attend free Html and Css courses conducted in wayup

After the very first lecture I started studying intensively because I was very interested

I completed the first stage in 2 weeks and mastered the basic knowledge of html and css. I did not stop there, I went through web design in wayup again, and as a code writing course I got a certificate here as well.

However I finally realized that my profession was web development and I started learning more thoroughly the computer languages you need to become a front end developer.

At this point I already know thoroughly the html and css, scss, bootstrap and web design software, figma and photoshop . But to fit everything into one profession I need javascript, which I am learning today
